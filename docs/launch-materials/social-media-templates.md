## Don'ts - What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't use fear-based marketing**

- "People are dying because they can't find help" ❌
- "We help people find critical services" ✅

❌ **Don't make unverifiable claims**

- "Best social services directory in Canada" ❌
- "Find 196+ verified services in Kingston" ✅

❌ **Don't share user data or stories without explicit permission**

- Testimonials require written consent
- Anonymize all usage examples

❌ **Don't engage with trolls or negativity**

- Respond professionally once
- Block/mute if harassment continues
- Don't get into arguments

❌ **Don't over-promote**

- Focus on value and community
- Share 80% helpful content, 20% promotion
- Avoid sounding like an ad
