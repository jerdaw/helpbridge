## Usage Guidelines

### Customization

All templates are **starting points** — customize as needed:

- Adjust tone to match your communication style
- Add specific metrics (user count, service count, etc.)
- Include local context (Kingston-specific news, events)
- Update with real testimonials and user stories

### Brand Consistency

When customizing:

- ✅ Keep color palette consistent (see Assets Guide)
- ✅ Maintain accessibility standards (WCAG AA)
- ✅ Use approved logos and assets
- ✅ Follow tone guidelines (empathetic, clear, community-focused)

### Approval Process

For major announcements (beta launch, public launch):

1. Review templates and customize
2. Share draft with stakeholders (if applicable)
3. Verify all links work (kingstoncare.ca)
4. Test social media previews
5. Schedule or post

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I modify these templates?

**A**: Yes! These are starting points. Customize tone, add specifics, update with real data. Just maintain brand consistency (see Assets Guide).

### Q: What if we don't have a designer?

**A**: See "Asset Creation Tools (Free Options)" in the Assets Guide. Canva and Figma have templates that work well. Start with critical assets (logo, favicon, OG image) and expand over time.

### Q: Which social media platforms should we prioritize?

**A**: For Kingston audience:

1. **Facebook** (strong local community groups)
2. **Instagram** (visual, younger demographic)
3. **Twitter** (crisis communication, news sharing)
4. **LinkedIn** (professional network, service providers)

Start with 2 platforms and expand as capacity allows.

### Q: How often should we post?

**A**: Beta launch: 2-3 posts/week
Public launch: 3-5 posts/week (first month), then 2-3/week ongoing

Quality > quantity. Better to post 2 great posts than 7 mediocre ones.

### Q: What if we get negative feedback on social media?

**A**: See "Response Templates" in social-media-templates.md. Key principles:

- Respond professionally and promptly
- Acknowledge the concern
- Offer to resolve (email, DM)
- Don't engage with trolls (one reply, then mute/block if harassment)

### Q: Do we need all the assets listed in the Assets Guide?

**A**: No. Start with "Critical" assets (logo, favicon, OG image). Add "Important" assets before public launch. "Nice-to-Have" assets can wait for post-launch or be skipped entirely.

### Q: Can we share the press kit publicly?

**A**: Yes! The press kit is designed for distribution. Share with:

- Media contacts
- Community partners
- Funders/sponsors
- Anyone asking "What is Kingston Care Connect?"
