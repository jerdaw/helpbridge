# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Last Updated:** February 2026

---

## General Questions

### What is Kingston Care Connect?

Kingston Care Connect is a free search engine that helps you find social services in Kingston, Ontario. We have information on about 200 services including food banks, crisis support, housing help, health services, and more.

**It's like Google, but only for social services in Kingston.**

---

### Is this an official government service?

**No.** Kingston Care Connect is a community project created by volunteers. We are **not** affiliated with:

- City of Kingston
- Province of Ontario
- Federal government
- Any specific social service agency

We gather publicly available information and make it easier to search.

---

### How much does it cost?

**Completely free.** There are no:

- Fees
- Subscriptions
- Ads
- Hidden charges

We will never ask for payment or credit card information.

---

### Do I need to create an account?

**No.** You can search without:

- Creating an account
- Giving your name
- Sharing your email
- Logging in

The platform is completely anonymous.

---

### How do I report incorrect information?

If you find wrong or outdated information:

**Email us:** feedback@kingstoncare.ca

**Please include:**

- Name of the service
- What information is wrong
- Correct information (if you know it)

We typically fix issues within 2-3 business days.

---

### Why isn't [specific service] listed?

We might not have listed a service for several reasons:

1. **We don't know about it yet** - Email us to suggest it
2. **It's outside Kingston** - We focus on Kingston services
3. **It's not a social service** - We focus on essential services (food, housing, health, crisis, etc.)
4. **It closed or merged** - We're updating our records

**To suggest a service:**
Email feedback@kingstoncare.ca with the service name, what they do, and contact information.

## Privacy Questions

### Do you track my searches?

**No.** We never see what you search for.

**How it works:**

- All searches happen **on your device** (in your browser)
- Your search terms never reach our servers
- No one can see your search history
- Not even us

This is called "client-side search" and protects your privacy completely.

---

### Is my data shared with anyone?

**No.** We don't collect your data, so we can't share it.

**What we DON'T collect:**

- Your searches
- Your location
- Your name or email
- Your browsing history
- Your IP address (in most cases)

**What we DO use:**

- Minimal technical cookies (to make the site work)
- Language preference (stored in your browser only)

---

### Can service providers see who searches for them?

**No.** Service providers cannot see:

- Who searched for them
- How many people searched for them
- What search terms people used
- When people viewed their information

All search activity is completely private.

---

### How can I verify you don't track me?

**Good question!** Here's how to check:

1. **Open source code:** Our code is public at github.com/jerdog/kingston-care-connect
   - Anyone can review it
   - Security experts have verified our privacy claims

2. **Browser dev tools:**
   - Press F12 in your browser
   - Go to "Network" tab
   - Search for something
   - You'll see searches don't send data to our servers

3. **Privacy audit:** We passed third-party privacy audits (documentation available on request)

## Data Questions

### How do you verify services?

We use a **3-level verification system**:

**Level 1 (L1) - Basic:**

- We confirmed the service exists
- We checked their website or phone number
- ⚠️ Information may not be up-to-date

**Level 2 (L2) - Vetted:**

- We contacted the service directly
- We confirmed hours, location, and eligibility
- ✅ More reliable information

**Level 3 (L3) - Verified:**

- Official partnership with the service
- They update their own information
- ✅ Most reliable information

**Look for the verification badge** on each service card.

---

### How often is information updated?

**Update schedule:**

- **High-priority services** (crisis, food, housing): Reviewed every 3 months
- **Other services:** Reviewed every 6 months
- **User reports:** Fixed within 2-3 business days

**Last full audit:** We note this at the top of each service's detail page.

**If information seems wrong:** Please report it so we can update it quickly.

---

### What do verification levels mean?

**L1 (Basic Verification):**

- ⚠️ Use with caution
- Always call first to confirm details
- Information may be outdated

**L2 (Vetted):**

- ✅ Good reliability
- We've confirmed details recently
- Still call to confirm eligibility

**L3 (Verified Partnership):**

- ✅ Best reliability
- Service updates their own info
- Most current information

**Tip:** Prefer L2 and L3 services when possible.

---

### Can I add a service myself?

**Not directly.** To protect data quality, we manually review all new services.

**To suggest a service:**

1. Email feedback@kingstoncare.ca
2. Include:
   - Service name
   - What they do
   - Who they help
   - Phone, email, website
   - Address (if physical location)

3. We'll review within 1 week and add if appropriate.

## Technical Questions

### Which browsers are supported?

**Fully supported:**

- ✅ Chrome (any recent version)
- ✅ Firefox (any recent version)
- ✅ Safari (iOS 12+, macOS 10.14+)
- ✅ Edge (Chromium version)

**Not supported:**

- ❌ Internet Explorer (all versions)
- ❌ Browsers from before 2020

**If the site doesn't work:**

- Update your browser to the latest version
- Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)

---

### Does it work offline?

**Yes!** The site works without internet after your first visit.

**How it works:**

1. **First visit** (with internet): The site downloads service data to your device
2. **Later visits** (without internet): Search works normally using saved data
3. **When online again:** Data updates automatically

**What works offline:**

- ✅ Searching
- ✅ Viewing service details
- ✅ Reading information
- ❌ Clicking website links
- ❌ Sending emails
- ❌ Live map directions

**This helps if you have:**

- Slow internet
- Limited mobile data
- Spotty WiFi

### What if I have accessibility needs?

**We designed this platform to be accessible to everyone.**

**Features for people with disabilities:**

**Visual impairments:**

- ✅ Works with screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
- ✅ High contrast support
- ✅ Text can be enlarged (browser zoom)
- ✅ Keyboard navigation

**Cognitive disabilities:**

**Hearing impairments:**

- ✅ No audio-only content

**If you find an accessibility issue:**
Email feedback@kingstoncare.ca and we'll fix it quickly.

---

### Why does it ask for my location?

**Short answer:** To show you services near you, sorted by distance.

**How it works:**

1. You click "Use my location"
2. Your browser asks permission
3. If you allow it, results are sorted by distance
4. **Your location never leaves your device**
5. We never see or store your location

**You can always say no.**

- Search works fine without location
- You just won't see distance sorting

**Privacy guarantee:**

- Your location is only used for sorting
- It's not sent to our servers
- It's not saved
- It's not shared with anyone

## Emergency & Crisis Questions

### What if I'm in crisis?

**If you are in immediate danger, call 911.**

**For mental health crisis:**

- **Call or text 988** (Canada Suicide Prevention Line)
- **Distress Centre Kingston:** 613-544-1771 (24/7)

**For domestic violence:**

- **Interval House:** 613-546-1833 (24/7)

**The platform will show you crisis resources** if you search for words like:

- "suicide"
- "abuse"
- "crisis"
- "help me"

---

### Can I use this to report abuse?

**No.** This is a search tool, not a reporting system.

**To report:**

- **Child abuse:** Call Children's Aid Society or 911
- **Elder abuse:** Call police (613-549-4660) or 911
- **Domestic violence:** Call Interval House (613-546-1833) or 911

**The platform helps you FIND services, not report to them.**
