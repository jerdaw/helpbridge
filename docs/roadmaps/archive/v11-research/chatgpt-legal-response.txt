Ontario Legal Information Centre / Centre d’information juridique de l’Ontario

    Phone: 1-844-343-7462 (toll-free)
    stepstojustice.ca
    stepstojustice.ca

    Website: centreinfojuridique.ca

    Description: This is a bilingual service operated by the Association of French-Speaking Jurists of Ontario (AJEFO), but it is available to all Ontarians in English or French. The Centre offers a free 30-minute consultation with a lawyer to discuss any legal question
    stepstojustice.ca
    . This can be done over the phone or via a virtual meeting. It is an information and referral service – the lawyer will provide general legal information, help clarify your situation, and refer you to appropriate services or next steps (but they do not represent you in court). Topics covered include all areas of law: family, employment, immigration, civil disputes, criminal law, etc. It’s a one-time session per legal issue. The service is confidential. Essentially, it’s a “legal advice hotline” open to everyone, with an emphasis on serving those who might not know where to turn.

    Who it serves: Anyone in Ontario, regardless of income or language. You can use this service if you don’t have a lawyer and want preliminary advice. It’s particularly useful for people in rural areas or those seeking French-language legal information (they guarantee French service). There is no intake test – it’s open to the public for a one-time consult. After your 30-minute session, if you need further help, they will direct you to resources (such as a local legal clinic, Legal Aid, the Law Society Referral Service, or a private lawyer referral).

    Hours: Appointments are typically available Monday–Friday during business hours (roughly 9:00 am – 5:00 pm). You must call or book online to schedule your free 30-minute phone appointment. Same-day appointments may be possible if slots are open. (No evening or weekend appointments noted.)

    Text/Chat: No. The service is delivered by scheduled phone call or online video call. Booking can be done through an online form on their website.
