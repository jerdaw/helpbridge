## Getting Started

### What is Kingston Care Connect?

Kingston Care Connect is a search engine for social services. It helps you find:

- **Food banks** and meal programs
- **Crisis support** (mental health, suicide prevention, domestic violence)
- **Housing help** (shelters, rental assistance)
- **Health services** (clinics, counseling, addiction support)
- **Legal aid** and immigration help
- **Employment** and education programs
- **Family services** (childcare, parenting support)

We have information on about **200 services** in Kingston.

### Who Can Use This?

**Everyone.** This service is free and open to the public.

You don't need to:

- Create an account
- Give your name
- Share personal information

### Who Made This?

This platform was created by community members who wanted to make it easier to find help in Kingston. It is **not** an official government website.

## How to Search

### Basic Search

**Step 1:** Click in the search box at the top of the page.

**Step 2:** Type what you need. Examples:

- "food"
- "mental health"
- "housing"
- "free lawyer"

**Step 3:** Press Enter or click the search button.

**Step 4:** See your results below.

### Search Tips

**Use simple words:**

- ✅ Good: "food bank"
- ❌ Too specific: "emergency food assistance program"

**Try different words if you don't find what you need:**

- "job help" OR "employment"
- "counseling" OR "therapy" OR "mental health"

**Use categories:**

- Click on category buttons below the search box
- Examples: Food, Crisis, Housing, Health

**Filter by hours:**

- Check "Open Now" to see only services that are currently open

**Search near you:**

- Allow location access to see services sorted by distance
- Or type your neighborhood (like "downtown Kingston")

### Crisis Searches

If you search for crisis words like **"suicide"** or **"abuse"**, you will see:

1. **A red banner** with emergency numbers
2. **Crisis services** at the top of results
3. **Immediate help options** (phone, text, chat)

**If you are in immediate danger, call 911.**

## Understanding Results

### Service Cards

Each search result shows a **service card** with:

#### 1. Service Name

The organization or program name.

#### 2. Description

What the service does and who it helps.

#### 3. Contact Information

- 📞 **Phone:** Tap to call (on mobile)
- 📧 **Email:** Tap to send email
- 🌐 **Website:** Click to visit their site
- 📍 **Address:** Get directions

#### 4. Hours of Operation

When the service is open. Examples:

- "Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm"
- "24/7" (always open)
- "Call for hours"

#### 5. How to Access (Access Script)

Step-by-step instructions:

- **Who is this for?** (eligibility)
- **What to bring** (ID, documents)
- **How to get help** (walk-in, call, referral needed)

#### 6. Verification Badge

Shows how we verified this service:

- **L1 (Basic):** We confirmed it exists
- **L2 (Vetted):** We contacted them directly
- **L3 (Verified):** Official partnership (best)

Higher levels mean more reliable information.

#### 7. Languages

Languages the service offers (English, French, etc.).

#### 8. Accessibility

Information about wheelchair access, parking, transit.

### What If There Are No Results?

If you see "No results found," try:

1. **Use different words**
   - "food" instead of "groceries"
   - "housing" instead of "shelter"

2. **Check your spelling**

3. **Browse categories** below the search box

4. **Remove filters** (like "Open Now")

5. **Search for a broader topic**
   - "health" instead of "diabetes clinic"

## Using the Map

### View Services on a Map

**Step 1:** Click the **"Map View"** button (top right of results).

**Step 2:** See pins showing where each service is located.

**Step 3:** Click a pin to see that service's details.

**Step 4:** Click **"Get Directions"** to open in Google Maps.

### Allow Location Access

To see services **near you**:

**Step 1:** Click **"Use my location"** or allow location when asked.

**Step 2:** Your browser will ask permission. Click **"Allow"**.

**Step 3:** Results will now be sorted by distance from you.

**Note:** Your location is **never** saved or shared. It only works on your device.

## Accessibility Features

We built this platform to work for everyone.

### Keyboard Navigation

You can use this site **without a mouse**:

- **Tab:** Move to the next button or link
- **Shift + Tab:** Move back
- **Enter:** Click a button or link
- **Spacebar:** Check a box
- **Escape:** Close a popup

### Screen Readers

This site works with screen readers like:

- JAWS
- NVDA
- VoiceOver (Mac/iPhone)
- TalkBack (Android)

**Screen reader tips:**

- Results are announced as you search
- Use headings to jump between sections
- Service cards have clear labels

### Text Size

Make text bigger or smaller:

- **In your browser:** Press `Ctrl +` (Windows) or `Cmd +` (Mac)
- **On mobile:** Use your device's accessibility settings

### High Contrast

If you have low vision:

1. Turn on **High Contrast Mode** in your operating system
2. The site will automatically adjust colors

### Reduced Motion

If animations make you dizzy:

1. Turn on **Reduce Motion** in your device settings
2. The site will show less animation

## Offline Mode

### What is Offline Mode?

The site works **even without internet**. This helps if you:

- Have a slow connection
- Are in a place with no WiFi
- Want to save mobile data

### How It Works

**First visit (with internet):**

1. Visit the site
2. The site saves service information on your device
3. You see a message: "Ready to work offline"

**Next visits (without internet):**

1. Visit the site (even offline)
2. Search works normally
3. You see results from saved data

**Note:** Offline data updates automatically when you go online.

### What Works Offline?

✅ **Works:**

- Searching for services
- Viewing service details
- Reading descriptions
- Seeing phone numbers and addresses

❌ **Doesn't work:**

- Clicking website links (needs internet)
- Getting live directions
- Sending emails
- Seeing real-time updates

## Privacy & Your Data

### We Don't Track You

**What we DON'T collect:**

- Your searches (we never see what you search for)
- Your location (stays on your device only)
- Your personal information
- Your browsing history
- Your IP address (in most cases)

**How search works:**

- All searches happen **on your device**
- Your search never goes to our servers
- No one can see what you searched for

### Open Source

This platform is **open source**. That means:

- Anyone can see the code
- Experts can check we're telling the truth
- You can verify we don't track you
- Code is at: github.com/jerdog/kingston-care-connect

### Cookies

We use **minimal cookies** only for:

- Remembering your language choice
- Making the site work (technical cookies)

We **never** use cookies for:

- Tracking you across sites
- Showing you ads
- Selling your data

### Is This Service Official?

**No.** This is **not** an official City of Kingston or government website.

We are a community project. Service information is:

- Collected from public sources
- Verified by volunteers
- Updated regularly
- Provided as-is

**Always verify** important details (like hours or eligibility) by calling the service directly.

## Common Questions

### Is this free?

**Yes.** This platform is completely free to use. No ads, no subscription, no hidden fees.

### Do I need to create an account?

**No.** You can search without signing up or giving any information.

### How do I report wrong information?

If you find incorrect information:

1. **Email us:** feedback@kingstoncare.ca
2. **Tell us:**
   - Which service has wrong info
   - What is wrong
   - What it should say (if you know)

We'll fix it quickly.

### Why isn't [my service] listed?

We focus on high-impact social services in Kingston. If a service is missing:

1. **Email us:** feedback@kingstoncare.ca
2. **Include:**
   - Service name
   - What they do
   - Contact information
   - Website (if they have one)

We'll review and add it if it fits our criteria.

### Can I use this on my phone?

**Yes!** The site works on:

- iPhones and iPads
- Android phones and tablets
- Any device with a web browser

It also works offline (see [Offline Mode](#offline-mode)).

### Which browsers work?

**Modern browsers:**

- ✅ Chrome (recommended)
- ✅ Firefox
- ✅ Safari
- ✅ Edge

**Older browsers:**

- ⚠️ Internet Explorer: Not supported
- ⚠️ Browsers from before 2020: May not work fully

### Is my data safe?

**Yes.** We don't collect your data, so there's nothing to leak or hack.

Your searches and location **never leave your device**.
